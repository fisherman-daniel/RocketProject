Other Notices

Reprinted with permission.

This book has been out of print and very hard to find for many years. For this reason, I decided to undertake the task of putting it into electronic media for wider dissemination. If you need to contact me about this book, please email me, magnus@mit.edu.

The book was scanned from a copy provided to me by the generosity and foresight of Wayne Gramlich of Sun. Numerous errors were introduced by the scanning and OCR process that I and other volunteers removed by hand. Special thanks goes to Jim Glass of Rocketdyne for his outstanding efforts.
